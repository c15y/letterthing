c15y

c15y (pronounced "see-fifteen-why") stands for countertechnology: "c" + 15 letters + "y" Countertechnology is the application of technology for reducing the harmful effects of technology. To get along in society everyone must conform to the machine interface, to behave like a machine themselves in order to get along with the machines. We move at the hyperspeed pace of technology, which ironically wastes our time with constant distractions. Online services, especially social media, vie for our attention, pushing information to influence what we should like, what we should read, what we should do, when, and with whom. c15y is countering this trend by creating some special technologies to help people live life on their terms, matching the form and cadence of the human interface. One important principle of countertechnology is reintermediation: putting a human mediator between you and the machine to serve as a helper, a messenger, a guide or just a shield.
